Privacy

This portfolio is designed to share professional work examples without collecting personal visitor profiles.

01 / Analytics

Anonymous usage information may be collected.

The site may use Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand general portfolio traffic. This can include page views, referral source, country or broad geographic region, device category, browser category, and a campaign code supplied through the optional ref URL parameter.

The optional ref parameter is treated only as a neutral campaign-source code. It is sanitized, kept out of canonical and social metadata, and is not stored in cookies or local storage by this site.

02 / Limits

The site is not intended to identify individual visitors.

Analytics on this portfolio are not intended to collect names, email addresses, personal profiles, cross-site behavioural data, browser fingerprints, keystrokes, or form contents.

No public resume or CV file is provided on this site. Full CV and references are available on request.
