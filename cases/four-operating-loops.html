Executive summary

Live service work needed clear control loops.

The operating challenge was fragmented visibility across demand signals, physical capacity, reorder logic, supplier/spec constraints, billing-state transitions, and shortage escalation. The portfolio case translates that operating work into a PM-readable model.

Operating context

EnvironmentAerospace manufacturing service delivery.Embedded technical operations support in a regulated production environment.
Scale5 buildings, 17 vending systems, 1,500+ active SKUs.Enough complexity to require structured controls and follow-up discipline.
StakeholdersPlant users, suppliers, receiving, and internal support.Cross-functional coordination was part of the operating load.
