Executive summary

I turned service noise into governable operating controls.

I worked in a live aerospace manufacturing service environment where floor issues, slot capacity, reorder timing, billing-state movement, vendor/spec constraints, and shortage escalation competed for attention. I separated those signals into visible control paths so the program could be managed by status, risk, ownership, and operating value instead of anecdotes.

Operating context and delivery pressure

Environment

Major aerospace manufacturing client.

Production support required service continuity, fast issue visibility, and disciplined supplier follow-up.

Scale

5 buildings · 17 vending systems · 1,500+ active SKUs · 30+ suppliers.

Enough complexity to require a managed operating system, not only manual follow-up.

Role

Sole on-site representative.

I had to connect plant users, receiving, suppliers, internal teams, data, and issue resolution into one operating picture.
