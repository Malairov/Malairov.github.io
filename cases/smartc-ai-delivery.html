Executive summary

Technical delivery and recovery work made visible.

The work involved AI-enabled transportation safety, hardware, computer vision/software behavior, QA validation, field integration, and sponsor-controlled governance. Pavlo's role was delivery coordination, visibility, readiness structure, and sponsor-facing control.

Problem / recovery context

Reliability and readiness gaps created delivery risk. Multiple technical streams had to be brought into one visible operating model so sponsors and delivery teams could understand what was ready, blocked, or decision-dependent.

Pavlo's PM role

CoordinatedDelivery rhythm and workstream visibility.Scope, risks, issues, dependencies, and readiness gaps.
SupportedSponsor-facing decisions.Status, readiness, prioritization, and change visibility.
