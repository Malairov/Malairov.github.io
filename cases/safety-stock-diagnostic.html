Executive summary

The issue was a control-system failure.

New SKUs with weak or no usage history were vulnerable to being treated as inactive or hidden. The risk came from several logic layers reinforcing the wrong outcome.

Problem and constraints

ConstraintNo usage history is not dead demand.
ConstraintWorksheet formulas can be overwritten.
ConstraintHardcoded filters are brittle.
ConstraintMacro version control matters.

Diagnostic lab

Select a failure mode to inspect how it affects operational visibility.

Failure 01

No usage history

A new SKU can be operationally important before usage history exists. Treating no history as dead demand hides the exception state.

Controls built

Power QueryMove fragile logic out of worksheet formulas.
NO-USAGECreate explicit exception state.
Dynamic headersReplace hardcoded field assumptions.
DiagnosticsAdd intermediate visibility columns.
