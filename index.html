03 Root cause Diagnosis

Safety-stock formula silently excluding new items

New SKUs were vanishing from reorder review. Traced the fault across three layers — a hub formula, the query logic feeding it, and a classification macro — to find new items being scored against thin usage history and misrouted to a dead-stock bucket.
