Operating approach

Control begins before the dashboard.

I start by defining what must become visible: the operating signal, decision owner, risk state, dependency, evidence, and closure condition. The tool follows the management logic—not the other way around.

  1. SignalFeedback, demand, defects, constraints, exceptions.
  2. RiskImpact, urgency, uncertainty, and dependency exposure.
  3. OwnershipDecision rights, action owner, escalation path.
  4. ExecutionSequence, gate, due date, evidence, handoff.
  5. LearningClosure data, root cause, preventive control.
